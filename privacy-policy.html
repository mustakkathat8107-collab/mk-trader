Your privacy is important to us.

MK Trader is an educational application only. It provides trading-related learning content for educational purposes. MK Trader does not provide financial, investment, or legal advice. No profit or income is guaranteed. Users are fully responsible for how they use the information provided in the app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the user's mobile number for login and authentication purposes. We do not collect name, email address, contacts, SMS content, call logs, location data, device media, or any personal files stored on the user's device.

3. Use of Data

The collected mobile number is used strictly for identity verification, session management, security purposes, and access control for purchased content. We do not sell, rent, or share user data with unauthorized third parties.

All payments made within the MK Trader app are processed only through Google Play Billing. No external or third-party payment systems are used. MK Trader does not store any card details, UPI information, or bank-related data. Payment handling and security are fully managed by Google Play.

5. Refund Policy

All purchases made within the app are final. Once a payment is successfully completed, it is strictly non-refundable under any circumstances. Any refund-related policies are governed by Google Play’s refund policy.

6. Account Access & Deletion

MK Trader provides users with full control over their account. Users can delete their account directly from inside the app using the available account delete option. Once an account is deleted, all associated user data is permanently removed and access to purchased content is revoked. Account deletion is irreversible.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

User data is retained only while the account is active. When a user deletes their account, the associated data is deleted from our systems within a reasonable time. We do not retain unnecessary or inactive user data.

8. Data Security

User data is securely stored using Firebase and Google services. Industry-standard security practices are implemented to protect user information. However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Notifications
